----------description-----------------
This UI presents a clean, user-friendly mobile banking experience with a consistent maroon-and-white color palette that reinforces brand identity and trust. 
The home screen focuses on quick accessibility, offering clearly labeled action buttons for core tasks like accounts, payments, and settings, followed by a prominent promotions card to highlight offers without overwhelming the user. 
The second screen emphasizes discoverability, using tabs, search, and well-spaced cards to browse promotions easily, each supported by simple icons and readable text hierarchy. 
Overall, the layout feels balanced and intuitive, with ample white space, rounded components, and a fixed bottom navigation that ensures smooth movement between key sections of the app.
